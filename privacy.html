Privacy Policy

Business and compliance information for Ninja PIE Media.

This Privacy Policy explains how Ninja PIE Media, LLC collects and uses information submitted through this website, email, social links, forms, and collaboration inquiries.

Information we may collect

  • Contact details, stage name, legal name when required for documentation, social handles, email address, and message contents.
  • Collaboration information such as concept interests, experience level, availability, boundaries, and preferred platforms.
  • Technical information such as basic analytics, browser type, pages visited, and security logs from hosting providers.

Use of information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, evaluate potential collaborations, coordinate projects, maintain consent and compliance records, protect rights, and operate the website.

Confidentiality

Private identity details, unreleased content, addresses, payment details, and project terms are treated as confidential. A separate signed NDA or collaboration agreement may be required before sensitive details are shared.

Retention

Inquiry records may be retained as needed for business, legal, tax, compliance, consent, and safety purposes. Records associated with adult content compliance may require longer retention under applicable law.

Third-party services

This site may use Cloudflare Pages, Proton email routing, FormSubmit or a similar form backend, Postimages, and social/creator platforms. Each third-party service has its own privacy practices.
