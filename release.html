Model Release & Confidentiality Information

Business and compliance information for Ninja PIE Media.

No shoot, adult-oriented collaboration, monetized content, or publication should proceed without appropriate written agreements.

Typical documents

  • Model/performer release
  • Age and identity verification record
  • Content-use and publication agreement
  • Revenue-share or profit-pool agreement, if applicable
  • Confidentiality / NDA agreement
  • Platform-specific documentation where required

Confidentiality / NDA language placement

NDA expectations should appear in three places: the collaboration form checkbox, this Model Release page, and the actual signed NDA/collaboration agreement. The signed document—not the website alone—is what should protect private identity details, legal names, addresses, personal contact information, unreleased materials, and production details.

Boundaries and consent

Concept, wardrobe, boundaries, publication channels, compensation, usage rights, and takedown limitations should be discussed and documented before production begins.
